ecoSym: a 2-D life simulation

ecoSym is a 2-D life simulation, with a variety of parameters that can be altered by the sliders on the right.

Make a change, and hit the 'Reset' button to see the effect on the world. Or, click the buttons below to learn more.

    There are several settings that influence the world as a whole:

    These two cannot sum to more than 100:
    Rock Percentage
    The percentage of the world's cells occupied by rocks.
    Life Percentage
    The percentage of the world's cells occupied by life.

    These two must sum to 100:
    Herbivore Percentage
    The percentage of the living cells occupied by Herbivore.
    Plant Percentage
    The percentage of the living cells occupied by Plants.

    ×

    There are several settings that influence life, each having separate settings for Plants and Herbiores:

    Maximum Life Span
    The average maximum life span of the life form. Individuals have this maximum life span ± 5 turns (this prevents a mass simultaneous die-off of the initial organisms.
    Spontaneous Death Chance
    The organism's chance of dying spontaneously each turn. This is in addition to the risk of dying due to predation or to having reached the maximum life span.
    Growth Chance
    The organism's chance of growing in a given turn, provided it is growth-eligible (not yet at its maximum size).
    Spawn Chance
    The organism's chance of spawning in a given turn, provided it is spawn-eligible (large enough to span). size).
    Delay After Spawn or Growth
    The number of turns the organism must wait to Spawn or to Grow after having done either. size).
