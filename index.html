ecoSym: a 2-D life simulation

ecoSym is a 2-D life simulation.

Legend

Represent plants, in various sizes, from a spore to a fully mature plant.

Represent herbivores, in various sizes, from a calf to a fully mature herbivore.

Represent rocks, a barrier impenetrable to both plants and herbivores.

The world is pre-seeded with a certain percentage of the cells populated by rocks, and a certain percentage populated by life. Of the cells populated by life, some percentage is populated by plants, and some by herbivores.

All life forms in the world have various common properties (which may take different values for plants and herbivores). These include the chance that in any given turn they will

  • Grow
  • Produce offspring
  • Die
